# Furniture Discounting Erodes Profit
### Data Visualization & Storytelling — Superstore Sales Analysis

## Objective
Analyze Superstore sales data to uncover business insights around profitability, discounting, and regional performance, and present them as a clear visual story.

## Tools
Tableau Public

## Dataset
Sample Superstore dataset — 9,994 orders across Category, Sub-Category, Region, Sales, Profit, Discount, and Order Date.

## How to view
Download `data visualization and storytelling.twb` from this repo and open it in [Tableau Public](https://public.tableau.com/) or Tableau Desktop to explore the interactive dashboard and story.

## Key insights
- **Sales and profit are both trending upward** from 2014 to 2017, with sales growing faster than profit.
- **Tables and Bookcases are the only loss-making sub-categories** — Tables alone lost $17,725, likely due to high shipping costs relative to price.
- **Higher average discount rates correlate with lower profit** across sub-categories, explaining why heavily discounted furniture items underperform.
- **Sales are concentrated in a handful of states** — California, Texas, and New York drive a disproportionate share of total revenue.

## Recommendation
Reduce discounting on Tables and Bookcases, or re-evaluate shipping/pricing structure for those sub-categories to restore profitability.
